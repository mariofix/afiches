⭐ Editor's Pick

Flask-Login

The de-facto standard for user session management in Flask. Flask-Login handles the common tasks of logging in, logging out, and remembering your users' sessions. It keeps the user's ID in the session and lets you easily manage user authentication without tying your app to a particular database or permissions model.
